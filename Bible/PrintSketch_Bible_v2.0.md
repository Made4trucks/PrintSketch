# PrintSketch Bible v2.0

## 1\. Mission

**Mission**

PrintSketch does not create a generic truck.

PrintSketch recreates **YOUR truck**.

We build premium 3D printable wall art that captures the identity of a
specific truck, including the owner's custom accessories.

\---

## 2\. PrintSketch Classic Style

1. Design for the final 3D print, not for the screen.
2. White space is part of the design.
3. The cabin is always the hero.
4. Preserve the identity of the exact truck.
5. Preserve owner-installed accessories whenever possible.
6. Remove anything that does not improve the print.
7. Every line must have a purpose.
8. The design should feel light, elegant and premium.
9. The artwork should resemble premium laser-cut industrial wall art.
10. The owner should instantly recognize their own truck.

\---

## 3\. Design Rules

### Printing

* RULE\_001 -- Reference printer: Bambu Lab P1S
* RULE\_002 -- Nozzle: 0.4 mm
* RULE\_003 -- Layer height: 0.2 mm
* RULE\_004 -- Minimum printable line: 0.6 mm

### Project

* RULE\_005 -- European trucks only (MVP)
* RULE\_006 -- Tractor units only
* RULE\_007 -- Remove trailers automatically
* RULE\_008 -- Cabin is the most important element
* RULE\_009 -- Truck fills about 90% of the circle
* RULE\_010 -- Preserve photo perspective
* RULE\_011 -- Print quality is more important than screen appearance

### Details

* RULE\_012 -- Simplify headlights but preserve identity
* RULE\_013 -- Simplify the grille but preserve brand character
* RULE\_014 -- All text must remain readable
* RULE\_015 -- Remove details that do not improve the print
* RULE\_016 -- Every printable element must be strong enough for a 0.4
mm nozzle

### Style

* RULE\_017 -- Prefer white space over black fills
* RULE\_018 -- Black defines the truck, never dominates it
* RULE\_019 -- Do not place decorative text at the bottom of the circle
* RULE\_020 -- Prefer clean outlines over large black areas
* RULE\_021 -- Every black area must have a purpose
* RULE\_022 -- The artwork should feel light and elegant

### Identity

* RULE\_023 -- Never remove accessories that make the truck unique
* RULE\_024 -- Owner-installed accessories are more important than
factory micro-details
* RULE\_025 -- Preserve unique features before simplifying
* RULE\_026 -- If two details compete, keep the more characteristic one
* RULE\_027 -- Never add logos, stickers or graphics that are not
present on the truck
* RULE\_028 -- Keep the windshield mostly white with black outlines and
wipers

## 5\. Photo Requirements

* Sharp image
* Whole cabin visible
* Front not blocked
* Preferred angle: Front 3/4
* Daylight
* No heavy shadows
* Minimum 1500 px
* No filters
* Background is irrelevant
* Accessories must be visible
