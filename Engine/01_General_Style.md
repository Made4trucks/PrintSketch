GENERAL STYLE

Create a premium black-and-white industrial wall decoration designed specifically for FDM 3D printing.

The final artwork must look like a finished decorative product, not a photograph.

Use only solid black and white.

No grayscale.

No gradients.

No shadows.

No textures.

No realistic rendering.

No sketch effect.

White space is an intentional part of the design.

The final artwork should feel clean, elegant and premium.

The artwork should resemble premium laser-cut industrial wall art rather than a black illustration.
