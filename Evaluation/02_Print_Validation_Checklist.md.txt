# PRINT VALIDATION CHECKLIST
Version: 1.0

Purpose:
Final checklist before sending artwork to 3D printing.

Printing method:
White circular base first.
Black artwork printed on top.

This means floating black elements are allowed,
because they are supported by the white base.

---

## 1. Base

□ White base included  
□ Base thickness: 2–3 mm  
□ Circular shape correct  
□ Outer border clean  

---

## 2. Black Artwork

□ Black artwork sits on top of the white base  
□ No unwanted background  
□ Black shapes are clean  
□ No excessive black masses  

---

## 3. Line Thickness

□ Main lines are strong enough  
□ Thin details are not fragile  
□ Minimum visible line: 0.6 mm  
□ Important lines will survive slicing  

---

## 4. Text

□ Main brand readable  
□ Company name readable  
□ License plate readable if important  
□ Tiny decorative text removed or simplified  

---

## 5. Lights

□ Round lights simplified  
□ No tiny reflector patterns  
□ No thin spokes inside lamps  
□ Rectangular headlights simplified  
□ Light identity preserved  

---

## 6. Grilles and Mesh

□ Dense mesh simplified  
□ No extremely tiny openings  
□ Grille identity preserved  
□ Bullbar/guard printable  

---

## 7. Visual Check

□ Truck recognizable in 2 seconds  
□ Looks like customer’s truck  
□ Cabin is main focus  
□ Looks premium  
□ I would print this  

---

## Decision

□ NEEDS CLEANUP  
□ REGENERATE
