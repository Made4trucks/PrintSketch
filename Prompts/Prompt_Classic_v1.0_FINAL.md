Transform the uploaded European truck photo into a premium black-and-white circular wall decoration designed specifically for FDM 3D printing.

GENERAL STYLE

Create a clean, elegant, premium industrial design.

The result must look like a professionally designed wall plaque, not a photo.

Use only solid black and white.

No grayscale.

No shadows.

No gradients.

No sketch effect.

No realistic rendering.

White space is an intentional design element.

COMPOSITION

Keep the original camera angle and perspective.

Place the truck inside a perfect circular frame.

The truck should occupy approximately 90% of the circle.

Remove the complete background.

TRUCK IDENTITY

Preserve the exact truck brand and model.

Keep the characteristic shape of the cab.

Keep the identity of the grille.

Preserve the original grille geometry, including the exact number, spacing and proportions of horizontal grille bars whenever they are clearly visible.

Keep mirrors and windshield.

Do not redesign the truck.

Preserve owner-installed accessories whenever possible.

Preserve all unique truck identity elements, including sun visor lettering, company names, custom banners, model badges, auxiliary lights, grille lights, roof lights and other owner-specific visual features whenever they are clearly visible in the original photo.


TEXT

Make all important text larger and cleaner.

Brand name must be clearly readable.

Model designation must be clearly readable.

Company name should be large and balanced.

License plate should remain readable but simple.

Never invent or replace visible text.
If text cannot be reproduced accurately, simplify it but never change it.

DETAIL LEVEL

Design for a 0.4 mm nozzle.

Minimum printable line width about 0.6 mm.

Avoid extremely thin lines.

Avoid floating elements.

Every important element should connect naturally with the design.

HEADLIGHTS

Simplify internal headlight details.

Keep the characteristic shape.

Avoid tiny circles and very small decorative lines.

GRILLE

Keep the grille recognizable.

Reduce unnecessary small openings.

Prefer larger bold shapes.

PRINT STYLE

Use black only where it improves readability after 3D printing.

Clean white separation lines.

Balanced contrast.

Smooth curves.

Vector-like appearance.

The design must look excellent after 3D printing, not only on a screen.

NEGATIVE PROMPT

No background.

No scenery.

No people.

No buildings.

No road.

No trees.

No reflections.

No textures.

No dirt.

No random decorative patterns.

No fragile details.

No additional logos or graphics outside the truck.

STYLE DNA

The final artwork should resemble premium laser-cut industrial wall art rather than a black illustration.

The artwork should contain approximately 70–80% white space and 20–30% black elements.

Use black only to define the truck shape.

Avoid large solid black areas.

The windshield must remain mostly white with only thin black outlines, window frame and wipers.

The final artwork should feel clean, balanced and suitable for premium FDM 3D printing.

The truck itself must always be the main visual element.

TRUCK PRIORITY

The truck cab is the hero of the artwork.

If a trailer is present, completely remove it or reduce it to a minimal rear chassis.

Never allow the trailer to dominate the composition.

The cab should occupy approximately 70% of the truck itself.
